## 📊 The Four Pillars Framework

### I. FINANCIAL PHYSICS (25% weight)
**Question**: Does the machine work efficiently regardless of what it produces?

**Filters**:
- ✅ Consistent ROE >15% (last 10 years)
- ✅ Gross Margin >40% and stable (last 10 years)
- ✅ Positive Free Cash Flow (every year, last 5 years)
- ✅ "One Dollar Test" - $1 retained earnings creates ≥$1 market value
- ✅ Low Maintenance CapEx (<5% of revenue)
- ✅ Operating Margin >15% and stable
- ✅ Depreciation <10% of Gross Profit

### II. THE MOAT (30% weight)
**Question**: Is the business protected from competition?

**Filters**:
- ✅ Pricing Power - can raise prices above inflation
- ✅ Not a Commodity (unless lowest-cost producer)
- ✅ Un-Copyable Advantage (network effects, ecosystems, complex systems)
- ✅ High Switching Costs (customer lock-in)
- ✅ Understandability (can explain business in one sentence)

### III. MANAGEMENT & CULTURE (20% weight)
**Question**: Is leadership rational, honest, and evolutionary?

**Filters**:
- ✅ Candor in Failure - CEO admits mistakes in annual reports
- ✅ Resists Institutional Imperative - no hype-driven pivots
- ✅ Skin in the Game - insider ownership >5%
- ✅ Systemized Evolution - evolves before crisis
- ✅ Rational Capital Allocation - buybacks when cheap only

### IV. RISK & RESILIENCE (15% weight)
**Question**: Can the company survive bad seasons?

**Filters**:
- ✅ Interest Coverage >5x
- ✅ Recession Resilience - profitable during 2008 & 2020
- ✅ Uncorrelated Revenue - no single client >50%
- ✅ Risk of Ruin Zero - 12 months cash reserves
- ✅ Debt Payoff <4 years of earnings
- ✅ Debt-to-Equity <0.5 (adjusted for buybacks)
